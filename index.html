Welcome to Marcella

Marcella Restaurant is a culinary boutique located in the heart of Paseo Caribe, San Juan, Puerto Rico. We offer a unique dining experience focused on creative Creole and international gastronomy.

Our philosophy emphasizes the use of fresh, locally sourced ingredients from Puerto Rican farmers and producers, creating a true farm-to-table experience that celebrates the region's rich culinary heritage.
